Our Story

Starting with the adoption of a few dogs, 16 years back, now impacting more than 18000 animal lives.

A dog that was brought to use just was barely breathing as it met with an accident. The dog was bleeding like anything, bones protruding out of the skin and injured all over the body. Doctors and ward boys struggled a lot to save that innocent creature for 2 hours and finally, the operation went successful. Adequate care, medication and food were provided. After a month, On seeing that dog recovered and playing so well with other dogs, licking the care takers. We cannot explain how that sight was.

Such a kind of love and compassion, keeps us going. Like us, they have the right the lead a happy and struggle free life. Unfortunately, many of them are being abused, hurt or abandoned. We are here as a voice for them. We strive maximum to impact more and more lives. Our journey would be more meaningful if many people join their hands for this mission of saving our animal friends.
